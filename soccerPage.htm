The very beginning of my soccer journey started with recreational soccer. I was a proud member and teammate of the "Clean Air Kickers." My next season, out of two recreational leagues, I played on the "Black Widows." Rec soccer is a time to decide if one really is passionate about a sport, and for me, I had a positive experience.
The next step after recreational soccer, is competititive soccer. My first club team I have ever played on was the DMCV Sharks. I played for the Sharks up until 2019. I had good memories and good friends here throughout the span of many years. I had many good coaches on Sharks and had very nice and friendly teammates.
After Sharks, I decided it was time for a change, so I joined the competititive soccer team, "Albion SC." I am currently on the Albion DPL team and love my team and coach. We play on very nice fields, and I feel like I am progressing from game to game and practice to practice. I can't wait to see where this regional-travel team will take me.
